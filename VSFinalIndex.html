John Rambo

Former U.S. Army Special Forces Operator and Troubled Veteran


Rambo utilizing explosive arrows against a Hind helicopter

Personal History and Service Summary:

  • Jul 6, 1947 - Born "John Rambo" in Bowie, Arizona
  • Jan 1965 - Graduated Rangeford High School
  • Jan 1966 - Begins military service.
  • Sept 1966 - Deployed to South Vietnam in Sept 1966
  • 1967 - Returned to U.S. in 1967 and trained with U.S. Army Special Forces at Fort Bragg, N. Carolina under Colonel Trautman.
  • 1969 - Re-deployed to Vietnam as part of a Special Forces LR Recon Patrol code-named 'Baker Team'.
  • Nov 1971 - Captured alongside other Baker Team operators by North Vietnamese. The following years of imprisonment and torture would kill all but two members of Rambo's team.
  • May 1972 - Escaped captivity, then immediately re-deployed by his own volition.
  • Sept 17, 1974 - Officially discharged from the military
"...Rambo is the best combat vet I've ever seen. A pure fighting machine, with only a desire to win a war that someone else lost. And if winning means he has to die, he'll die - No fear, no regrets. And one more thing, what you choose to call hell, he calls home."
―Colonel Sam Trautman to Marshall Murdock.
